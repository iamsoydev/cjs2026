Player is a druid from a nearby tribe of druids who now live in the occupied
lands of ___. The presence of the settlers here have come with both benefits
and threats to these druids. Part of their cooperation is the ability to 
continue to raise livestock. Druids offer their services to these settlers
for traded goods, weapons, and favor. 

The tribal conflicts and wars have seen many of the men off to war. The young
and woman have remained and while the number of men in the tribe diminish at
a great rate, have still found it profitable to continue to work with the 
settlers for weapons and food to sustain the tribe.

A young girl is hired by a farmer to help him deal with the troubles amongst
the animals on the ranch and quell a defiance the animals have taken up 
against him. Under the rule and structure created by the farmer on this ranch
distrust, backstabbing and lies are all to prevelant.
